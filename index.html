⚠️ Key Pre-Processing Challenges

Fixing Delayed Status Errors:

We found 18 trips marked as 'Delayed' or 'Cancelled' even though their actual arrival time was exactly on time; we fixed these errors using a SQL query to set their status back to 'On Time' and clear the wrong delay reasons.

Standardizing Messy Text Fields:

The delay reasons columns were inconsistent, using different terms for the same thing like 'Signal failure' vs 'Signal Failure' or 'Staffing' vs 'Staff Shortage'; we unified all text categories so they group correctly in the dashboard charts.

Calculating Hidden Refunds & Profit:

The raw data only showed the ticket price without accounting for financial losses from train disruptions; we created a dynamic calculation rule based on UK rail logic to estimate refunds (from 5% to 100% back based on delay minutes) to find the true net profit.

Handling Midnight Trips (Overnight Durations):

For trains running around midnight, subtracting departure time from arrival time created broken negative numbers (like -1400 minutes); we solved this by adding a condition (+1440 minutes) to fix the journey duration for all overnight trips.

Breaking Down Ticket Discounts:

Ticket prices didn't show a clear breakdown of how marketing discounts affected the revenue; we engineered custom columns to reverse-engineer the original prices and separate the exact discount amounts (30% for Railcards, 50% for Advance, and 25% for Off-Peak).

Strategic Output

Business Recommendations

Based on our exhaustive analysis, we outline four tactical interventions for UK Rail operators.

01. Dynamic Pricing Strategy

Redistribute peak demand volumes using tactical off-peak discounts, optimizing passenger convenience while maximizing occupancy.

02. Infrastructure Reinforcement

Target maintenance funds specifically to mitigate weather anomalies and signaling faults, which account for over 50% of delay instances.

03. Streamlined Delay Claims

Integrate automatic claim systems into ticketing portals, significantly reducing user friction and restoring passenger satisfaction.

04. Centralized Data Infrastructure

Deploy a continuous pipeline that streams and fuses operational timetables with accounting records for real-time analytics.
